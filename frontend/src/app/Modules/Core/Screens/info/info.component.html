Philosophy

We imagine Wood Fired Oven Pizza should be served anywhere, in those small bar/café which cannot have the room for the cooking equipment, to your local Deli, in a fine-dining gourmet restaurant, in the local hotel while patrons are having their drinks after work, or simply at your own place with your beloved ones!
Just top it with your favourite ingredients straight from frozen and re-heat for as less as 5 mins(circa) at 180/200°.

Da Salva for Home:

For your home celebrations with the family, or you just came back from work but too tired to cook?
Maybe you have that Da Salva pizza in the freezer that can satisfy your hunger.
Our goal is to give you the same artisanal experience of an hand-made pizza served in the best pizzeria in your area! This goal was achieved through the innovative cryogenesis system thanks to which a freshly baked and still steaming pizza undergoes a blast chilling process in just a few minutes that takes it from a temperature of 90 ° C to -20 ° C.
This is how we bring to your home, unaltered, all the fragrance, aromas and unmistakable flavor of the True Neapolitan Pizza just out of the oven.
You can buy your favorite flavors online and always have our pizzas close at hand in the freezer, to be awakened in the oven for about 5 minutes and enjoyed whenever you want comfortably at home!

Storage & Cooking Instructions

Product can be stored for as long as 3 months if kept frozen, otherwise for up to 5 days or the expiry date shown on the package
To cook the pizza you simply need to pre-heat you oven at 180/200 degree then place your pizza into the oven and cook for 5min circa or until crust become soft
We suggest to cook the pizza from frozen
