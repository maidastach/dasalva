About Us

Da Salva is a Sydney based project to brings the Authentic Pizza Napoletana to businesses and homes. Da Salva's Pizza are approx 9" and prepared in the Traditional way and cooked with Imported Italian Wood Fired Oven. The pizzas are processed just after the cooking, freezing the product to keep all the nutritional values, aromas and fluffy softness until re-heated. Our testing with Qualified Chefs mixed with High Health and Safety Standards took us a result where the pizza will taste like just came out of a Wood Fired Oven. The dough is prepared with Italian flour, the High Hydration followed to a long Raising makes the Da Salva's pizza light and so easily digestible. Every Da Salva's pizza is stretched by hand and rich in flavours with a hint of San Marzano's Tomato Sauce.
